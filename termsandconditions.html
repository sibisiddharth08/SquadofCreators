1. Eligibility and Domain Requirements:

a. All members must choose at least one technical domain.
b. Members must select a total of two domains as indicated in the registration form.

2. Individual Credits and Recognition:

a. No individual credits will be publicly disclosed until the company's name gains recognition.
b. Contributors will be tagged in project announcements, and individuals can repost with their respective roles if needed.

3. Decision-Making Authority:

a. All new ideas and contributions must be discussed within the team.
b. Final decisions regarding projects and contributions rest with the board members.

4. Work Allocation and Progress Sharing:

a. Each member is required to actively work within their chosen technical domain.
b. Regular progress updates must be shared within the Squad of Creators WhatsApp group.
c. Doubts and queries regarding the chosen domain can be clarified through the group.

5. Intern Performance Recognition:

a. Interns' performance will be monitored regularly.
b. The "Best Intern of the Month" award will be given based on outstanding contributions and dedication.

6. Confidentiality and Public Disclosures:

a. Members are prohibited from publicly disclosing specific individual contributions to a project.
b. Public communication should focus on the collaborative effort of the team.

b. Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action, including termination of membership.
